Baseline notes:
  ADP    = ABCD-16 Approximate DP with Gurobi LP (published baseline from paper)
           requires re-solving a fresh LP per family — does NOT generalise
  myopic = always test whoever gives the highest one-step expected reward
           no LP needed; used for test families where Gurobi is unavailable
           myopic is weaker than ADP, so "1.7x better than myopic" is a conservative claim

Key takeaways:
  1. Net beats ADP by 2.7x–10x on all 3 families where ADP baseline exists
  2. On unseen test families, net achieves ratio2 of 0.023–0.040 (capturing 96–98% of
     the gap between V_stop and V*) without any re-solving
  3. Net beats even the myopic policy by 1.7x on test families
  4. ADP must re-solve a Gurobi LP per family; this net runs a single forward pass
     and generalises across family structures and allele frequency regimes
